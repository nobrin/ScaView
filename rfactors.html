Processing

  • The R value for merging all intensities in this data set.
    • Rmerge = Rsym = ∑hkl ∑i I⁡i hkl - I⁡hkl ∑hkl ∑i I⁡i hkl
    where I is the observed intensity and <I> is the average intensity of multiple observations of symmetry-related reflections.
    http://www.iucr.org/__data/iucr/cifdic_html/2/mmcif_pdbx.dic/Ireflns.pdbx_Rmerge_I_all.html
  • The redundancy-independent merging R factor value Rr.i.m., also denoted Rmeas, for merging all intensities in this data set.
    • Rmeas = Rr.i.m. = ∑hkl Nhkl Nhkl-1 ∑i I⁡i hkl - I⁡hkl ∑hkl ∑i I⁡i hkl
    http://www.iucr.org/__data/iucr/cifdic_html/2/mmcif_pdbx.dic/Ireflns.pdbx_Rrim_I_all.html
  • The precision-indicating merging R factor value Rp.i.m.
    • Rp.i.m. = ∑hkl 1 / Nhkl-1 1/2 ∑i I⁡i hkl - I⁡hkl / ∑hkl ∑i I⁡i hkl
    • Rp.i.m. = ∑hkl 1 Nhkl-1 ∑i I⁡i hkl - I⁡hkl ∑hkl ∑i I⁡i hkl
    where N is the redundancy (the number of times reflection hkl has been measured).
    http://www.iucr.org/__data/iucr/cifdic_html/2/mmcif_pdbx.dic/Ireflns.pdbx_Rpim_I_all.html

Refinement

  • Rwork = ∑ Fobs - Fcalc ∑ Fobs , where Fobs and Fcalc are the observed and calculated structure factors, respectively.
  • Rfree is calculated as Rwork using 5% of all reflections randomly chosen and excluded from structure calculation and refinement.
